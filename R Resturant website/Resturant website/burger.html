Cheese Burger

A burger is a popular fast-food item made up of a patty, usually of ground beef, chicken, or a vegetarian alternative, placed inside a bun. It is typically served with a variety of toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, pickles, onions, and sauces (such as ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise). Burgers can be customized to suit individual tastes and have become a staple in global cuisine.
